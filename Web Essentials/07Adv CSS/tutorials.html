Macrame
An art done by tying knots in specific patterns. This can be done to make jewlery, wall hangings, plant hangers, and other decor. (See Macrame Tutorials)
Embroidery
An art done by decorating fabrics with needle and thread. (See Embroidery Tutorials)
Various Knots
Withing Macrame and other knot tying arts, there will be many different types of knots including hitches, square knots, and many many more. When you know many of these knots you can let your imaginiation make the designs for you.
